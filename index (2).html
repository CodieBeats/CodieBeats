Who is CodieBeats?

CB
CodieBeats
Independent Music Producer & Beat Maker
CodieBeats is an independent music producer from the Philippines who has been crafting original instrumentals and beat productions since 2016. Known for blending raw emotion with hard-hitting rhythms, Codie creates beats that speak before words are even written over them.

With a passion for Trap, Lo-Fi, R&B, Drill, and cinematic soundscapes, CodieBeats has developed a unique sonic identity — dark, moody, melodic, and always radio-ready. Whether you're a rapper looking for your next anthem, a singer searching for the perfect canvas, or a content creator needing premium background music, CodieBeats delivers.

What CodieBeats Produces

From hard-hitting trap bangers to soulful lo-fi instrumentals — here's the sonic universe CodieBeats operates in.

🔥
Trap Beats
Hard 808s, rolling hi-hats, dark melodies. Built for rappers and hype culture.
🌙
Lo-Fi / Chill
Warm vinyl textures, soft drums, jazzy chords. Perfect for study, focus and relaxation.
💜
R&B / Soul
Smooth progressions, lush pads, emotional leads. Ideal for singers and songwriters.
🥊
Drill Beats
Sliding 808s, eerie keys, punchy snares. Dark, gritty energy straight from the underground.
🎬
Cinematic
Orchestral elements, tension-building dynamics, epic drops. Film and content creator gold.
🌊
Afrobeats / Amapiano
Groovy percussion, log drum bass lines, vibrant energy. Global sound, rising tide.

Melodic Rap
Catchy hooks, layered synths, radio-ready structure. Where melody meets street.
